## Notes

- Single client kernel that mutates total XP (`+0x730`) and runs level-up/down loops.
- S2C `GiveXP` (`Client_AwardKillExperience`) calls with **`PacketOrNonKill`** → kill-path spree/weapon bonus **skipped**.
